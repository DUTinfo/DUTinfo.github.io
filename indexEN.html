Training introduction

The DUT is a training framed and vocational wich takes place on 2 years with 2 semesters in a year. Every semester is split in 2 education units, with a IT part and a traditional part.

How have our diploma ?


The DUT is a diploma obtain with the continuous assessment. For have your diploma, is necessary to have an average of your years upper of equal at 10/20.
For validate a year, is necessary to have an average upper than 10/20. The average of the year is composed with the average of the 2 semesters of the year.
For validate a semester, is necessary to have an average of the 2 education units upper than 10/20 without any average of an education unit less than 8/20.

Why choose this training ?


The strength of this training, is the pedagogic supervision, she is share in TP/TD, magistral courses and semester project wich permited a diversity of the learnings.
The DUT permits different options, the start of the professional life with a bac+2 level or the continued education with the obtaining of the diploma.

What does the BUT IT ?


From the 2021 academic year, the IUT innovate, with a new training, the BUT.
You can suscribe in BUT in Parcoursup, the BUT is available to the genral bachelors, technilogics and profesionnals. The admission is based on file.

What is the BUT IT ?


The BUT IT is a 3-year training course that replaces the DUT IT that was put in place until now.
The new version of the DUT is validated after 2 years of BUT.
The learning process does not change, it always revolves around the implementation of professional situations through the implementation of tutored projects and different experiences in companies through internships or work-study. The alternation can be set up at the beginning of the training, or triggered during the training at the beginning of a year.

What are the differences between the BUT and the DUT ?


The BUT and the DUT have much in common, but there are some notable differences.

The duration of the initial training which increases from 2 years to 3 years, which leads to an increase in the number of ECTS credits from 120 ECTS credits to 180 ECTS credits.

Another difference to note is that the BUT can be integrated at different stages of the CV thanks to inter-training gateways.

And finally, the BUT will be complemented by a compulsory English certification.

What to do at the end of the BUT?


Following the BUT, it is possible to envisage a continuation of studies in an engineering school, master. It is also possible to return to professional life at the end of your BUT.

The opportunities


At the exit of the DUT, several options are proposed to you.
At the passage to the second year, two choices are proposed, the choice of long or short studies.

The short studies


After obtaining your DUT, it is possible to enter the professional world.
Upon graduation from the IUT, students can become assistant engineers or project managers.
Upon completion of the DUT, students are operational in computer development, design and implementation of computer systems according to specifications, ... which allows them a wide variety in the choice of jobs.

The long studies

Continued education


After the DUT, 80% of students decide to continue their studies, several choices are available to them.
